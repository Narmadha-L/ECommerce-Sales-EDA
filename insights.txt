Insight 1 — November Peak
November 2011 had the highest revenue of £1.5 Million — nearly double the average monthly revenue.
This spike is driven by Black Friday and early Christmas shopping.
Business should increase inventory and run targeted marketing campaigns from October onwards.

Insight 2 — UK Market Concentration
United Kingdom contributes approximately 85% of total revenue showing heavy geographic concentration.
Business should consider expanding marketing in Netherlands and EIRE which show next highest revenue potential.

Insight 3 — Top 2 Products Dominate
"PAPER CRAFT LITTLE BIRDIE" leads with 80,000+ units sold.
Top 2 products alone contribute disproportionately high sales volume — these should never go out of stock.

Insight 4 — Low Value Orders Pattern
Majority of orders fall between £0–£50 showing this is a high volume, low value B2C business.
Focus should be on increasing average order value through bundling or upselling strategies.

Insight 5 — Strong Q4 Seasonality
Revenue consistently grows from August, peaking in November.
Q4 alone contributes nearly 40% of annual revenue — making it the most critical quarter for this business.
